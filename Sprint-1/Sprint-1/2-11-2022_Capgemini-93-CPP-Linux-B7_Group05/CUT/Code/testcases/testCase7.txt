Project Title=Researcher.Life Ambassador of the Month: January 2022
Abstract=The Researcher.Life Ambassador Program is a global community of researchers who wish to give back to their academic 
communities through mentoring and collaboration with other researchers. Researcher.Life Ambassadors empower themselves and their 
fellow researchers with advanced tools and services from Researcher.Life that help them do more, be more, and succeed more. 
Every month we spotlight one Ambassador who has gone the extra mile in supporting their academic community.
